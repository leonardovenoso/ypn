Making a difference today

The Young Philanthropists Network (YPN) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization and the parent company of the Young Professionals Network which was founded in 2006. YPN is dedicated to providing education, infrastructure and support to disadvantage communities globally.

Over the years, YPN has participated in numerous Giving activities from fundraising, to supporting local establishments and volunteering overseas to make a difference. The majority of our 7,000 plus members are Silicon Valley based where they has supported charities focused on helping the underprivileged through donations and hands-on support. Donations have gone out to support Chile, Japan, Philippines, Sunday Friends, American Red Cross and many other charities, communities and countries.

During this holiday season, YPN encourages you to make a difference in the lives of others by making a small contribution. Your tax deductible charitable donations help countless others livelihood and to gain a sustainable future.
